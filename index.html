Our Story

Founded in 2019, Chavonn Spices was born during the uncertainty of the lockdown with a simple yet powerful purpose ‚Äî to bring authentic, homemade spices back to everyday kitchens. What began on a very small scale, preparing masalas at home with care and honesty, quickly found love among people seeking purity, taste, and tradition in their food. Chavonn is a heartfelt journey started by a mother and son, rooted in family values, trust, and age-old recipes. Each blend was crafted just like it would be for our own home ‚Äî fresh, natural, and free from shortcuts. Step by step, with the support and faith of our customers, we grew steadily, staying true to our promise of quality over quantity. In 2025, Chavonn Spices marked a major milestone by completing all official registrations and proudly evolving into a D2C (Direct-to-Consumer) brand. Today, we operate two physical stores ‚Äî one in Kowad and another in Virar, Mumbai ‚Äî bringing us closer to the communities we serve. Having already served countless households, our journey is far from complete. Our mission is clear: to serve authentic, homemade Indian spices to every kitchen, preserving traditional flavors while maintaining modern standards of hygiene and trust.
